About Me

Hi there, I'm Moin Gani and I am a web developer based out of Toronto. After graduating with an Accounting degree and working in the financial services industry, I gained valuable life experience and was ready to take the next step into a world of creative freedom and lifelong learning. I quickly fell in love with web development and have since been passionate about providing the best user experience possible with a conscious eye for accessibility. When I'm not coding, I enjoy travelling and experiencing new cultures, anxiously following my favourite Toronto sports teams, and goofing around with my 4-year old nephew.
